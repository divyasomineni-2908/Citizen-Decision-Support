# Real Indian Government Schemes Database

## Overview
This document provides detailed information about the 30+ real Indian government welfare schemes that have been integrated into GovWelfare Connect. Each scheme is based on actual government programs designed to support citizens across various categories.

---

## Agricultural Schemes

### 1. **Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)**
- **Department**: Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare
- **Eligibility**: Landholding farmers (no income/age restrictions)
- **Benefits**: ₹6,000/year in 3 installments
- **Application**: pmkisan.gov.in
- **Impact**: Covers 11+ crore farmer families nationwide

### 2. **Jal Jeevan Mission**
- **Department**: Ministry of Jal Shakti
- **Eligibility**: Rural households
- **Benefits**: In-house water connections with 24x7 supply
- **Application**: jaljeevanmission.gov.in
- **Impact**: Ensuring safe drinking water to all households

## Healthcare & Social Security Schemes

### 3. **Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PM-JAY)**
- **Department**: Ministry of Health & Family Welfare
- **Name**: Ayushman Bharat
- **Eligibility**: EWS & economically weaker sections
- **Benefits**: ₹5 lakhs health cover/year for hospitalization
- **Application**: pmjay.gov.in
- **Coverage**: 10.74+ crore families

### 4. **Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)**
- **Department**: Ministry of Labour & Employment
- **Eligibility**: 18-70 years
- **Benefits**: ₹2 lakhs accidental death, ₹1 lakh disability
- **Premium**: ₹12 annually (auto-debited)
- **Application**: pmsby.gov.in

### 5. **Atal Pension Yojana (APY)**
- **Department**: Ministry of Labour & Employment
- **Eligibility**: 18-40 years (unorganized sector)
- **Benefits**: Guaranteed pension ₹1,000-₹5,000/month after 60
- **Government Co-contribution**: Up to 50% of contribution
- **Application**: atalpensionyojana.gov.in

### 6. **Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-dhan Scheme (PM-SYM)**
- **Department**: Ministry of Labour & Employment
- **Eligibility**: Unorganized sector workers, 18-40 years
- **Benefits**: Monthly pension ₹3,000 after age 60
- **Contribution**: ₹55-₹200/month (subsidized)
- **Application**: maandhan.in

## Women & Child Development

### 7. **Sukanya Samriddhi Account Scheme**
- **Department**: Ministry of Women & Child Development
- **Eligibility**: Girl child under 10 years
- **Benefits**: High interest rate, tax benefits under 80C
- **Minimum Investment**: ₹1,000 annually
- **Application**: indiapost.gov.in

### 8. **Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao Scheme**
- **Department**: Ministry of Education & Women Development
- **Eligibility**: Female students
- **Benefits**: Educational scholarships, community awareness, skill training
- **Application**: betibachaobetipadhao.gov.in

## Housing Schemes

### 9. **Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) - Urban**
- **Department**: Ministry of Housing & Urban Affairs
- **Eligibility**: EWS, LIG (max income ₹18 lakhs)
- **Benefits**: ₹1-₹2.50 lakhs subsidy, interest subsidy up to 6.5%
- **Application**: pmayuclap.gov.in

### 10. **Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Gramin**
- **Department**: Ministry of Rural Development
- **Eligibility**: Rural poor
- **Benefits**: ₹1.20-₹1.30 lakhs construction assistance
- **Application**: pmayg.nic.in

## Employment & Skill Development

### 11. **Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA)**
- **Department**: Ministry of Rural Development
- **Eligibility**: Rural residents, 18+ years
- **Benefits**: 100 days guaranteed paid employment/year
- **Wage**: ₹202/day (2024)
- **Application**: nrega.nic.in

### 12. **Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)**
- **Department**: Ministry of Skill Development
- **Eligibility**: Students & workers, 15+ years
- **Benefits**: Free industry-relevant skill training, certification
- **Application**: pmkvyofficial.org

### 13. **Pradhan Mantri Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)**
- **Department**: Ministry of MSME
- **Eligibility**: 18+ years, entrepreneurs
- **Benefits**: Subsidy ₹10-₹15 lakhs for micro-enterprises
- **Application**: pmegp.gov.in

## Education Schemes

### 14. **National Scholarship Portal (NSP)**
- **Department**: Ministry of Education
- **Eligibility**: Students (school to PhD)
- **Benefits**: Merit-based, merit-cum-means, caste-based scholarships
- **Categories**: SC/ST, OBC, Minorities, Disabilities
- **Application**: scholarships.gov.in

### 15. **Post Matric Scholarship for SC/ST**
- **Department**: Ministry of Social Justice
- **Eligibility**: SC/ST students post-matriculation
- **Benefits**: Tuition fee reimbursement, maintenance allowance
- **Application**: scholarships.gov.in

### 16. **Mid-Day Meal Scheme**
- **Department**: Ministry of Education
- **Eligibility**: School children up to 14 years
- **Benefits**: Free cooked meal on all school days
- **Impact**: Improved nutrition & school enrollment
